3 key Elements define Japandi Interior design


Simplicity and minimalism

Japandi design is characterized by its simplicity and minimalism. Clean lines are hallmarks of this design style, allowing for a serene and calming atmosphere.

Natural materials

Use natural materials such as wood, stone, and bamboo. Incorporating these materials in your Japandi interior not only adds warmth and texture but also creates a strong connection to nature.

Contrast and balance

Mixing the light, airy feel with the more grounded and earthy tones creates a harmonious and visually appealing space.
