Everything You Need to Know About Japandi Interior Design




A combination of Japanese and Scandinavian aesthetics, the look is calm, casual, and organic

Though centered on tranquil, minimalist, and functional interiors, the aesthetic often permeates into more than just the look of a home.
This design style and principles truly supported a healthy meaningful lifestyle.
It teaches us to find beauty in imperfection, form deep connections to the earth and nature, and enjoy the simple pleasures of life.





JAPANDI INTERIORS



How Japanese and Scandinavian Styles Work Together

If you're familiar with Scandi design, you're sure to have come across the notion of "hygge." This is the Scandinavian concept of coziness in design. Basically, hygge means that your home should be your sanctuary and provide a feeling of comfort every time you walk in the front door.


Incorporate Natural Materials

Focus on natural materials such as unfinished woods or bamboo pieces that bring in the feeling of nature and simplistic beauty. I recommend using muted colors with hints of pale green or bringing plants and greenery into your home to give it a sense of outdoor living.
